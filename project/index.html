Zurila

Impacting Lives through Pocket Change

Smart Spending

We are striving to help people through source crowdfunding of pocket change. We want to give people of all walks of life the same opportunity. We want to revitalize and bring value to pocket change. Majority of people are inconvenienced by pocket change and see no value in them. Zurila attempts to teach the value in the little things, no matter how big or small.

How it Works:

A weighted monthly drawing from the pool of donors with prizes that is dependent on donations. Prizes are divided 50% towards the winner of the pool and the other 50% to the charity chosen.

Partners

Zurila partners with multiple organizations and charities and splits the prize pool with them, truly making impactful changes one step at a time.
